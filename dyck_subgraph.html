The Dyck subgraph is the induced subgraph on the Cn vertices satisfying the ballot condition. Its edges are adjacent transpositions 10↔01 that preserve Dyck-ness. This is precisely the Tamari lattice Tn — the 1-skeleton of the associahedron Kn+1.

Consecutive k-Cycles Permutation Graphs

Consecutive k-cycles: Cyclic permutations (i, i+1, i+2, ..., i+k-1) for i=1,...,n-k+1.
For k=2: neighbor transpositions (Coxeter generators of Sn)
For k=3: 3-cycles generating An (alternating group)
Even k generates Sn, odd k generates An (even permutations only)

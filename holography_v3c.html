      Holography map: Given a binary vector (graph node), read left to right — each 1 = step UP, each 0 = step RIGHT. This gives a bijection from graph nodes to lattice paths on the k × (n−k) rectangle.

      Graph edges: two nodes are connected if an adjacent transposition (i, i+1) sends one vector to the other.

      Complexity = Area: The graph distance (minimum adjacent transpositions) between two nodes equals the area between their corresponding paths. This is the Mann-Whitney U statistic = area between ROC curves.

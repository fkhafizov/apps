All Vertices

Each vertex simultaneously represents a Dyck path, a triangulation of an (n+2)-gon, a parenthesization of n+1 symbols, and a binary tree with n internal nodes. The edges correspond to adjacent transpositions 10↔01 preserving the ballot condition — equivalently, diagonal flips in the triangulation, or single applications of the associativity rule (xy)z → x(yz).
